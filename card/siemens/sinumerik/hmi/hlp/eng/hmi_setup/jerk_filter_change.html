Changing jerk filters

In this dialog, you change the parameters of the selected jerk filter:

  • FIR low-pass: Frequency and tolerance (for linear axes only)

  • FIR low-pass ADV: Frequency and tolerance

  • Double moving averaging: Time constants 1 and 2

  • Moving average calculation: Time constant

⇒ Additional actions:

  • Press the "Cancel" softkey to cancel the inputs.

  • Press the "Accept" softkey to accept the filter settings defined for the axis.
