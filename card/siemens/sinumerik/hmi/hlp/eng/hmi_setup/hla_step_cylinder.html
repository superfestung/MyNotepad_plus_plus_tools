Hydraulic linear drives: Configuration - cylinder data

In this dialog, you enter the cylinder data and define the safety circuit for the cylinder.

In addition to the piston diameter (p0310), the rod diameter of the A and B side (p0311, p0312) must be entered. With a differential cylinder, the rod diameters are different. One of the two rod diameters can also be zero. The maximum piston stroke (p0313) and the cylinder dead volume (p0314, p0315) are also required.

The cylinder dead volume is the liquid volume between the cylinder and servo solenoid valve which cannot be displaced by the piston. The dead volume affects the natural frequency of the drive.

You can enter the following "cylinder data":

  • Cylinder piston diameter [mm]

  • Cylinder piston rod diameter A side [mm]

  • Cylinder piston rod diameter B side [mm]

  • Cylinder piston stroke [mm]

  • Cylinder dead volume A end [cm3]

  • Cylinder dead volume B end [cm3]

Using the "Manipulated variable inversion" option, you can invert the output voltage of the hydraulic module. The output voltage of the hydraulic module is the input voltage of the valve.

You configure the safety circuit of the cylinder under "Safety circuit". You have the following setup options:

  • Close shutoff valve for power disable (PD)

  • Switch off servo solenoid valve supply for power disable (PD)

  • Valve spool checkback present

  • Invert valve spool checkback
