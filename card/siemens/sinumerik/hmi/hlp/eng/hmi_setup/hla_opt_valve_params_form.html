HLA characteristic: Valve characteristic parameters

The valve characteristic parameters are displayed in a list.

The parameters displayed in the list are read from the drive. Write the changed values to the drive with the ENTER key.

Note

Only change the parameters during a pulse inhibit

Do not change the parameters in the "U" (Run) drive state because this can have unforeseeable effects on a traversing axis.

The following parameters are listed:

  • Velocity limit, positive direction

    Setting of the maximum velocity for the positive direction.

    Value: 0.000 m/min to 1000.000 m/min; factory setting: 1000.000 m/min

  • Velocity limit, negative direction

    Setting of the maximum velocity for the negative direction.

    Value: -1000.000 m/min to 0.000 m/min; factory setting: -1000.000 m/min

  • Velocity controller controlled system gain

    Setting of the velocity controller controlled system gain.

    Value: 0.0 mm/Vmin to 20000.0 mm/Vmin; factory setting: 0.0 mm/Vmin

  • Positive area adaptation factor

    Setting of the factor for the area adaptation in the positive direction.

    Value: 10.0% to 200.0%; factory setting: 100.0%

  • Negative area adaptation factor

    Setting of the factor for the area adaptation in the negative direction.

    Value: 10.0% to 200.0%; factory setting: 100.0%

  • Knee compensation, Q1, positive zero range

    Setting of the flow rate Q for point 1 positive (zero range) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.01% to 95.00%; factory setting: 0.01%

  • Knee compensation, U1, positive zero range

    Setting of the voltage U for point 1 positive (zero range) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 95.00%; factory setting: 0.00%

  • Knee compensation, rounding 1, positive zero range

    Setting of the rounding for point 1 positive (zero range) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 20.00%; factory setting: 0.00%

  • Knee compensation, Q1, negative zero range

    Setting of the flow rate Q for point 1 negative (zero range) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.01% to 95.00%; factory setting: 0.01%

  • Knee compensation, U1, negative zero range

    Setting of the voltage U for point 1 negative (zero range) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 95.00%; factory setting: 0.00%

  • Knee compensation, rounding 1, negative zero range

    Setting of the rounding for point 1 negative (zero range) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 20.00%; factory setting: 0.00%

  • Knee compensation, Q2, positive

    Setting of the flow rate Q for point 2 positive of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.20% to 95.00%; factory setting: 10.00%

  • Knee compensation, U2, positive

    Setting of the voltage U for point 2 positive of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 95.00%; factory setting: 10.00%

  • Knee compensation, rounding 2, positive

    Setting of the rounding for point 2 positive of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 20.00%; factory setting: 2.50%

  • Knee compensation, Q2, negative

    Setting of the flow rate Q for point 2 negative of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 95.00%; factory setting: 10.00%

  • Knee compensation, U2, negative

    Setting of the voltage U for point 2 negative of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 95.00%; factory setting: 10.00%

  • Knee compensation, rounding 2, negative

    Setting of the rounding for point 2 negative of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.00% to 20.00%; factory setting: 2.50%

  • Knee compensation, Q3, positive saturation

    Setting of the flow rate Q for point 3 positive (saturation) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.20% to 100.00%; factory setting: 100.00%

  • Knee compensation, U3, positive saturation

    Setting of the voltage U for point 3 positive (saturation) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.20% to 100.00%; factory setting: 100.00%

  • Knee compensation, Q3, negative saturation

    Setting of the flow rate Q for point 3 negative (saturation) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.20% to 100.00%; factory setting: 100.00%

  • Knee compensation, U3, negative saturation

    Setting of the voltage U for point 3 negative (saturation) of the knee compensation.

    Value: 0.20% to 100.00%; factory setting: 100.00%

Further actions:

  • Softkeys "Axis +" / "Axis -" to change to the next/previous axis.

  • Softkey "Select axis >" to open a dialog in order to select the axis from a list.

  • Softkey "Select DDS" to open a dialog to select the drive data set (DDS) whose parameters are to be displayed in the list.

    The selected drive data set does not apply for the measurement of the characteristic. The measurement is always performed with the drive data set that is active in the drive at the time of the measurement.

    The drive data set to which the dialog refers is displayed at the far right of the window title, e.g. DDS0.

  • Softkey "Copy characteristic" to open a dialog to select the drive data sets to which the characteristic parameters are to be copied.

  • Softkey "Back" to return to the "Measure valve characteristic" dialog.
