Hydraulic linear drives: Overview of connection data

The connection data read out of the valve and cylinder are displayed in the connection data overview.

The data read out of the supply unit is displayed in the supply unit overview.

The data read out of the shutoff valve is displayed in the shutoff valve overview. The following outputs are possible for the shutoff valve:

  • No shutoff valve

  • Axis-specific shutoff valve

  • Central shutoff valve
