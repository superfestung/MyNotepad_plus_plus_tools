Drive devices - inputs/outputs

This overview shows the terminal assignment of those drive units (NCU and NX) involved in the SINAMICS drive line-up.

Further actions:

  • The "Drive unit +", "Drive unit -" or "Select drive unit... " softkeys are only displayed if more than one drive unit exists in the system.

  • Using the "Assign terminal" softkey, you open a dialog to interconnect the selected terminal.
