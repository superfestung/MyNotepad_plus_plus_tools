Hydraulic linear drives: Configuration - connection data

You can configure the following "connection data":

  • Cylinder mass

    The total mass moved by the hydraulic drive (e.g. table, tool, workpiece).

  • Cylinder mounting position A side

    The mounting position in relation to the A side of the cylinder in degrees. The mounting position of the cylinder (p0344) specifies to what degree the force due to the weight of the moved mass (p0341) is taken into account in calculating the servo gain and maximum travel-in/travel-out speed.

  • Line length A side

    Length of the hydraulic line between valve and cylinder on the A side in meters.

  • Line length B side

    Length of the hydraulic line between valve and cylinder on the B side in meters.

  • Inner line diameter

    Inner diameter of the hydraulic lines between valve and cylinder for the A and B sides in millimeters.

You can select from the following options under "Cylinder mounting type":

  • At the cylinder barrel

    The piston is stationary, the moved mass is attached to the cylinder.

  • At the piston rod

    The cylinder is fixed, the moved mass is attached to the piston rod.

You can select from the following options under "Connection configuration valve-cylinder":

  • Valve A to cylinder A

    Valve side A is connected to cylinder side A.

  • Valve A to cylinder B

    Valve side A is connected to cylinder side B.
