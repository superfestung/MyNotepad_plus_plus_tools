Drives: Equivalent circuit diagram for motor data, synchronous

The following motor data required to complete the configuration and identification of the motor:

p0350[0]

Setting of the stator resistance of the motor at ambient temperature p0625.

p0354[0]

Setting of the rotor/secondary part resistance of the motor at ambient temperature p0625.

For separately excited synchronous motors: Setting of the damping resistance in the direction of the rotor (d-axis).

p0356[0]

Asynchronous machine, separately excited synchronous machine: Setting of the stator stray inductance of the motor.

Synchronous machine: Setting of the stator cross inductance of the motor.

p0358[0]

Setting of the rotor/secondary part stray inductance of the motor.

For separately excited synchronous motors: Setting of the damping inductance in the direction of the rotor (d-axis).

p0360[0]

Setting of the main inductance of the motor.

For separetely excited synchronous motors: Setting of the saturated main inductance in the direction of the rotor (d-axis).

Further actions:

  • Alternatively you can also have the data calculated. If the information is not available, press the "Calculate ESB data" softkey and the parameters for the equivalent circuit diagram are calculated using the motor model.

  • Select "Finish" and the parameters are then calculated. The progress for this process is provided in the status bar. You can then save the non-transient changes (→ "OK") or cancel them (→ "Cancel").
