Encoder data - Encoder type Digital (abs. + INCR.)

The encoders offered by Siemens can be selected from a list during the drive configuration. With the selection of the encoder type, all the necessary parameter settings are automatically transferred into the encoder configuration and they can no longer be modified.

The following settings must be configured:

  • Encoder type

  • Inversion

  • Resolution (rotary encoder only)

Absolute encoder rotary multi-turn resolution

p0421[0..n]

Absolute encoder rotary single-turn resolution
