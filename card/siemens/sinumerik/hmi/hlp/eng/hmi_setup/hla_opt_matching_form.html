HLA characteristic: Alignment

The following requirements must be satisfied before the three adjustments are performed:

  • Position adjustment

    The axis must be referenced and the piston must be at position A (DE endstop).

  • Pressure equalization

    The system must be in the no pressure state.

  • Valve offset adjustment

    The axis must be in closed-loop control operation.

To perform the adjustments, press the "Position adjustment", "Pressure adjustment" and "Valve offset adjustment" softkeys in this sequence. The progress of the respective adjustment is displayed.

If required, change the offset values for the pressure sensors, the D/A conversion and the piston zero point.

Further actions:

  • "Axis +" / "Axis -" softkeys to change to the next/previous axis.

  • "Select axis >" softkey to open a dialog in order to select the axis from a list.

  • Perform the piston position adjustment with the "Position adjustment" softkey. The value for position A is accepted. The softkey is disabled if the requirements for the adjustment are not satisfied.

  • Perform the adjustment for the pressure sensors with the "Pressure adjustment" softkey.

  • Perform the adjustment for the valve control voltage with the "Valve offset adjustment" softkey.

  • "Back" softkey to return to the "Measure Valve Characteristic" dialog.
