Drives: Asynchronous motor data

The following motor data is required mandatorily for configuration and identification of the motor:

p0304, p0305, p0307, p0308, p0310, p0311, p0322, p0335, p0604, p0605

Further actions:

  • After a change is made to the motor data a re-calculation (p0340) of the filter and control parameters is requested:

    p0340 = 0: No calculation ⇒"No" softkey

    p0340 = 1: Complete recalculation of the control parameters with the commissioning data ⇒ "Yes" softkey

  • As an option you can enter the data of the equivalent wiring diagram. Activate the option and press the "Next step >" softkey.
