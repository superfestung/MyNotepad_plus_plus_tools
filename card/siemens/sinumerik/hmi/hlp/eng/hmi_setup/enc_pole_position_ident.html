Encoder data - Identify pole position

Important parameters are displayed here depending on the procedure for pole position identification. In the selection list select the pole position identification process (p1980) so that only the parameters that are part of this process are displayed.

The following processes can be selected:

 

  • Saturation-based 1st + 2nd harmonic

(p1980 = 0)

  • Saturation-based 1st harmonic

(p1980 = 1)

  • Saturation-based 2-level

(p1980 = 4)

  • Motion-based

(p1980 = 10)

  • Elasticity-based

(p1980 = 20)

Pole position identification

For synchronous motors, the pole position identification determines its electrical pole position, that is required for the field-oriented control. Generally, the electrical pole position is provided from a mechanically adjusted encoder with absolute information.

A one-off identification of the pole position is required for motors with an encoder that has not been balanced or calibrated.
