HLA characteristic: Measurement parameters

The measurement parameters are displayed in a parameter list.

The parameters displayed in the list are read from the drive. You can adapt all parameters to your requirements. Write the changed values to the drive with the ENTER key.

The following parameters are listed:

  • HLA positive control voltage limit

    Setting of the limit for the positive control voltage.

    Value: 0.0 V to 10.0 V; factory setting: 10.0 V

  • HLA negative control voltage limit

    Setting of the limit for the negative control voltage.

    Value: -10.0 V to 0.0 V; factory setting: -10.0 V

  • Valve identification voltage: Start of measuring range

    Setting of the voltages at the start of the measuring range.

    Value: 0.0 V to 10.0 V; factory setting: 0.0 V

  • Valve identification voltage: End of measuring range

    Setting of the voltages at the end of the measuring range.

    Value: 0.0 V to 10.0 V; factory setting: 10.0 V

  • Valve identification voltage: Positive travel

    Setting of the voltages for travel in the positive direction.

    Value: 0.0 V to 10.0 V; factory setting: 2.0 V

  • Valve identification voltage: Negative travel

    Setting of the voltages for travel in the negative direction.

    Value: 0.0 V to 10.0 V; factory setting: 2.0 V

  • Valve identification measurement path: Minimum

    Setting of the range for the minimum measurement path.

    Value: 0.0% to 100.0%; factory setting: 10.0%

  • Valve identification measurement path: Maximum

    Setting of the range for the maximum measurement path.

    Value: 0.0% to 100.0%; factory setting: 90.0%

  • Number of measuring points

    Number of measuring points along the entire characteristic.

    Value: 0 to 1000; factory setting: 100

  • Standstill detection, encoder lines

    Standstill of the axis is detected when the parameterized number of encoder lines has not been crossed within the standstill time.

    Value: 0 to 1000; factory setting: 4

  • Valve identification times: Ramp time

    The voltage is changed from 0 ... 10 V within this time. The ramp time is effective for all voltage changes.

    Value: 0.00 s to 100.00 s; factory setting: 0.20 s

  • Valve identification times: Settling time

    After the target voltage is reached, this period is waited before the measurement starts.

    Value: 0.00 s to 100.00 s; factory setting: 0.20 s

  • Valve identification times: Measurement time

    The velocity is averaged within this time.

    Value: 0.00 s to 100.00 s; factory setting: 0.10 s

  • Valve identification times: Standstill time

    Period waited when there is no motion before the next measurement.

    Value: 0.00 s to 100.00 s; factory setting: 4.00 s

  • Valve identification times: Wait time

    Period waited between two measurements in order to fill the pressure accumulator. The value applies for the maximum velocity and is reduced internally depending on the velocity.

    Value: 0.00 s to 100.00 s; factory setting: 4.00 s

Further actions:

  • Softkey "Measure >" to open the "Measurement" dialog. You can start a measurement of the valve characteristic there. After the measurement, the measured characteristic and the characteristic set in the drive are displayed in a diagram.

  • Softkey "Back" to return to the "Measure valve characteristic" dialog.
