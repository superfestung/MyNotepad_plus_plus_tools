Axis: Parking position

Display the actual axis position to check that when starting the measurement, the axes do not travel to the end stop.

  • A black arrow indicates the current position of the axis.

  • A white arrow with number indicates the position to which the axis was traversed in a previous tuning.

No settings are possible in this dialog: To do this, change to the "Machine" operating area in order to traverse the axes to a suitable, safe position.

Further actions:

  • "Clear history" softkey to delete the file in which the tuning strategy, history and the user-defined settings of this axis are stored.

    The values of the automatic servo tuning are reset to the default values: The options, the strategy settings and the measurement parameters for the axis are also reset. The status line in the axis overview is deleted. The controller parameters of the individual axes are retained.

  • "Cancel" and "OK" softkeys to cancel or continue the measurement.
