⇒ Further actions:

  • Softkey "Add signal" to add a signal.

  • Softkey "Delete signal" to delete a signal from the list.

  • Softkey "Load signals" to load signals from a file.

  • Softkey "Save signals" to save signals.

  • Softkeys "Cancel" / "OK" to cancel or confirm the action.
