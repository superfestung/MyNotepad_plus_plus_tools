Removing an external encoder

Procedure:

  1. In operating area, open "Commissioning" → Softkey "Drive system" → Softkey "External encoder":

  2. Select the encoder in the overview, and then press softkey "Remove encoder".

  3. The encoder data are removed from the system after acknowledging with "Yes". This procedure can take several minutes and is completed with the prompt to Reset (po).

  4. Remove the encoder from the DRIVE-CLiQ interface.

  5. Confirm with "OK".
