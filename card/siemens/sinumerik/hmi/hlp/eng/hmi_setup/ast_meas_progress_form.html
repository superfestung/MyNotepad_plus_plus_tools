Axis: Measurement is running

"Measurement" dialog

⇒ Further actions:

  • Softkey "Start meas." or "Stop meas." to start the next measurement of stop a measurement.

  • Softkey "Meas. parameters >" is only activated when a measurement has been completed. You then change to the parameter view in order to adapt the measurement parameters for the next measurement.

  • Softkey "Bode diagrams" to change from the parameter view to the frequency response display.

  • Softkey "Diagram >" to change to the diagram editing.

  • Softkey "Display legends" to hide the legends.

  • Softkey "Tuning log" to display the log about the progress of the tuning.

  • Softkey "Cancel" to cancel the measurement.
