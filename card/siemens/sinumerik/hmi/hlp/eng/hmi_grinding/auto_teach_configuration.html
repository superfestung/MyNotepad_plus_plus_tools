Settings for teach-in

In the "Settings" window, you define which axes are to be included in the teach-in block and whether motion-type and continuous-path mode parameters are to be provided.

Procedure

 

1.

Teach-in mode is active.

 

2.

Press the ">>" and "Settings" softkeys.

The "Settings" window opens.

 

3.

Under "Axes to be taught" and "Parameters to be taught", select the check boxes for the desired settings.

 

4.

Press the "Accept" softkey to confirm the settings.
