Creating machine protection areas

Stand under offset

 

1.

Mark the "ROOT" kinematic element and press the "New element" softkey.

 

2.

Press the "Collision element" softkey.

 

3.

In the "New collision element" window, select the collision element "Machine protection area" and press the "OK" softkey.

 

4.

Enter the following values:

  

Name:

Stand

  

Color:

Gray

  

Detail level:

0

  

PLC bit:

-1

  

Activation:

Activated

 

5.

Press the "OK" softkey to save the values.
