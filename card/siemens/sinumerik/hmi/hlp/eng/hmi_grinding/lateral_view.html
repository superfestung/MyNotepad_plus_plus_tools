Further side views - for surface grinding

Displaying further side views

 

1.

Simultaneous recording or simulation is started.

 

2.

Press the "Further views" softkey.

 

3.

Press the "From the front" softkey if you wish to view from the front.

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the "From the rear" softkey if you wish to view from the rear.

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the "From the left" softkey if you wish to view from the left.

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the "From the right" softkey if you wish to view from the right.

Changing the display

You can enlarge, reduce, pan, and rotate the simulation graphic and the segment.
