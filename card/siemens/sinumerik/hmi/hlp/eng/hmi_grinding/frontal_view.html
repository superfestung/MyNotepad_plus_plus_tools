Face view - for cylindrical grinding

Displaying the face view

 

1.

Simultaneous recording or simulation is started.

 

2.

Press the "Further views" and "Face view" softkeys.

 

 

The face view shows the workpiece in the X-Y plane.

Changing the display

You can enlarge, reduce, pan, and rotate the simulation graphic and the segment.
