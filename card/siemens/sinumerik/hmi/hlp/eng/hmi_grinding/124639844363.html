Channel switchover

It is possible to switch between channels when several are in use. Since individual channels may be assigned to different mode groups, a channel switchover command is also an implicit mode switchover command.

When a channel menu is available, all of the channels are displayed on softkeys and can be switched over.

Changing the channel

 

 

Press the <CHANNEL> key.

 

 

The channel changes over to the next channel.

 

 

- OR -

 

 

If the channel menu is available, a softkey bar is displayed. The active channel is highlighted.

Another channel can be selected by pressing one of the other softkeys.

Further information

For information on configuring the channel menu, refer to the SINUMERIK Operate Commissioning Manual.
