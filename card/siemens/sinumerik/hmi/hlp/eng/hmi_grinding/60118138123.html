Enlarging or reducing the CAD drawing

Requirement

The DXF file is opened in the Program Manager.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the "Details" and "Zoom +" softkeys if you wish to enlarge the size of the segment.

 
 

 

- OR -

 

2.

Press the "Details" and "Zoom -" softkeys if you wish to reduce the size of the segment.

 
  

- OR -

 

3.

Press the "Details" and "Auto zoom" softkeys if you wish to automatically adapt the segment to the size of the window.

 
  

- OR -

 

4.

Press the "Details" and "Zoom elem. selection" softkeys if you want to automatically zoom elements that are in a selection set.
