Creating a tool protection area

Tool under linear axis Z

 

1.

Mark the "Z axis" kinematic element and press the "New element" softkey.

 

2.

Press the "Collision element" softkey.

 

3.

In the selection box, select "Tool protection area" collision element and press the "OK" softkey.

 

4.

Enter the following values:

  

Name:

T

  

Color:

red

  

Detail level:

0

  

PLC bit:

-1

  

Activation:

Activated

  

TO unit:

1

  

Magazine:

9998.

  

Location:

1

 

5.

Press the "OK" softkey to save the values.
