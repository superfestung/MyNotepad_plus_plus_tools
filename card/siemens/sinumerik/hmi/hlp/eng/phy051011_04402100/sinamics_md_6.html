Description: Sets the overvoltage protection for synchronous motors in the field-weakening range.

Refer to: F07432, F07906, F07907
Note: In the field-weakening range, synchronous motors can, when a fault condition exists, generate high DC link voltages. The following possibilities exist to protect the drive system from being destroyed due to overvoltage:

- activating the internal voltage protection (IVP) with p1231 = 3.
