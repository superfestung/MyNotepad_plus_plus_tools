Creating a collision pair

Using collision pairs, you define the two protection areas, which should mutually monitor themselves regarding collision.

Precondition

A kinematic chain has been created and includes valid protection areas.

Procedure

 

1.

Position the cursor in the tree view on the "Collision groups" folder.

The "New collision element" selection window opens.

 

2.

Select "Collision pair" and press the "OK" softkey.

 

3.

Enter the values and press the "OK" softkey to confirm the entries.
