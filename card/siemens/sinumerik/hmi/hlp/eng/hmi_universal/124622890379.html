Creating an NC directory on the local drive

On the local drive, you have the option of mapping the directory structure of the NC memory. This also simplifies the search sequence.

Creating Directories

 

1.

The local drive is selected.

 

2.

Position the cursor on the main directory.

 

3.

Press the "New" and "Directory" softkeys.

The "New Directory" window opens.

 

4.

In the "Name" entry field, enter "mpf.dir", "spf.dir" and "wks.dir" and press the "OK" softkey.

The directories "Part programs", "Subprograms" and "Workpieces" are created below the main directory.
