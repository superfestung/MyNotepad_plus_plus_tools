Trace settings - procedure

Procedure:

 

1.

You are in the "Select Variables for Trace: ..." window. ...".

 

2.

Press the "Settings" softkey.

The "Trace Settings" window opens.

 

3.

Select the desired settings and enter the values.

 

4.

Confirm with "OK" to accept the settings.
