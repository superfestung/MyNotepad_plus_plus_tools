Displaying the IPC pairing certificate

Introduction

With the "View certificate" function, you can view the detailed information about the pairing certificate of the IPC. You can safely manage the IPC pairing certificate in the SINUMERIK certificate store.

Requirement

  • With active user management: You have the role "Security Admin" or access level 2 (service).
    OR

  • Without active user management: You have at least access level 3 (user).

  • An NCU is in "Paired" status.

Procedure

  1. In the "Setup" operating area, call the overview page for pairing with the softkeys "Security > Trusted devices".

  2. Select the IPC if a NCU has the status "Paired".

  3. Click on "View certificate".


    Pairing - viewing the IPC certificate

  4. The detailed information on the pairing certificate of the IPC is displayed.
