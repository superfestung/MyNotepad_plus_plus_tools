TRAORI_DYN

Requirement

You are on a KT element in the operating area "Start-up" → "NC" → "Transformations" → "New" and have selected the transformation type TRAORI_DYN - dynamic orientation transformation.

The dialog for the combinations opens.

Combinations of rotational axis to chain

Number of rotational axes in tool and workpiece chains: 0 to 3 are the only numbers permitted. The sum of the combination must be between 1 and 3.
