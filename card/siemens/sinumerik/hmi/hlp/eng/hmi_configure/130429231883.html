Overview

You must register your machine such that Siemens Support can better support your machine. The registration is relevant for servicing and additional support services.

You have the following options of registering the machine:

  • Using a machine-specific QR code

  • Manually via the registration portal with URL "www.siemens.com/sinumerik/register"

Registration using the QR code

The QR code is displayed after SINUMERIK Operate runs up.

Use the camera app on your mobile device to scan the QR code. After scanning the QR code, the URL of the registration portal opens in your browser.

After entering the data necessary for registration, a code is generated in the registration portal.

You then enter this code into SINUMERIK Operate for registration.
