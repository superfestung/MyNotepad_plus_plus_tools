Using the scroll function

The following table shows the different options for scrolling through the trace diagram using the keyboard:

Function

One step, normal speed

Continuously, normal speed

One step, high speed

Continuously, high speed

Scroll horizontally

  • Press left/right arrow once

  • Hold left/right arrow

  • Press <Shift> +
    left/right arrow once

  • Hold <Shift> +
    left/right arrow

Scroll vertically

  • Press up/down arrow once

  • Hold up/down arrow

  • Press <Shift> +
    up/down arrow once

    - OR -

  • Press <Page Up>/<Page Down> once

  • Hold <Shift> +
    up/down arrow

    - OR -

  • Hold <Page Up>/<Page Down>

To move the selected signal curve using the keyboard:

Function

One step

Continuously

Move individual signals up/down

Press <Ctrl + Up Arrow> once

Keep <Ctrl + Up Arrow > pressed

Move individual signals down

Press <Ctrl + Down Arrow > once

Keep <Ctrl + Down Arrow > pressed

If the selected signal is part of a signal group, all signals in the group are moved.

The following table shows the different options to scroll through the trace diagram using the mouse:

Function

Mouse

Scroll vertically, normal speed (single step)

  • Click arrows on vertical scrollbar

    - OR -

  • Move mouse wheel up/down over chart area or over the vertical scrollbar

    - OR -

  • Press and drag left mouse button up/down on empty area of chart (in "Move" mode)

Scroll vertically, higher speed (page step)

  • Click above/below the slider on the vertical scroll bar

Scroll chart horizontally, normal speed (single step)

  • Click arrows on horizontal scrollbar

    - OR -

  • Press <SHIFT> and move mouse wheel up/down over chart area or over the horizontal scrollbar

    - OR -

  • Press the left mouse button and drag left/right on an empty diagram area (in "Move" mode)

Scroll chart horizontally, higher speed (page step)

  • Click left/right of the slider on the horizontal scrollbar

Move one signal up/down individually

(If the selected signal is part of a signal group, all signals in the group will be moved)

  • Press left mouse button over the vertical scale of the signal and drag up/down (if the scale is visible)

    - OR -

  • Press left mouse button over the signal curve and drag up/down

    - OR -

  • Toolbar icon (click once or hold down for continuous scrolling)

Scrolling the trace diagram or a selected signal with touch gestures:

You can move the whole chart by touching an empty area of the chart and dragging the chart up/down or left/right. An individual signal curve can be moved up/down by dragging the signal curve or the vertical scale of the signal.
