Creating a logbook

The logbook provides a history of the machine in an electronic form.

The date that the machine is commissioned is entered into the logbook and if service is carried out at the machine, then this can be electronically entered. This allows service to be optimized.

Logbook entries can be changed or deleted up to the end of the 2nd commissioning.

Sequence

 

 

In the "Diagnostics" operating area, select the version display.

 

 

 
 

 

Open the logbook

 

 

Record the data on the machine identity using the "Change" softkey.

 

 

After the machine has been first commissioned at the machinery construction OEM, the commissioning date is documented using the "1st startup completed" softkey.

As a minimum, the machine name/No., the customer No. and the country where the manufacturer is located must be entered into the machine identity.

The "1.SETUP" entry is displayed in the "Machine logbook" window.

 

 

At regular intervals, you will be prompted to perform the 2nd commissioning and transfer the data to EUNA.

 

 

After an additional commissioning (start-up) by a dealer or service organization, the commissioning date is documented using the "2nd startup completed" softkey.

As a minimum, the country of the end customer must be entered into the machine identity.

The "2.SETUP" entry is displayed in the "Machine logbook" window.

 

 

You will be prompted to transfer the machine identity to EUNA.

 

 

Using the "New entry" sofkey, measures and fault descriptions are documented, specifying the name, company and department as well as a brief description.
