Context menu overview

The context menu can be opened from the trace diagram as follows:

  • Press the right mouse key

  • Press keys <Shift+F10> or <Ctrl+M> on the keyboard

  • Tap the touch panel with two fingers (tap finger gesture)

You can perform the following actions using the context menu:

  • Move

  • Zoom

  • Show and hide measuring cursor

  • Configure the vertical scales of signals

  • Arrange signals in tracks

  • Show and hide legend widget

  • Show and hide signal table

  • Modify colors of the trace diagram background, grid lines and horizontal scale.
