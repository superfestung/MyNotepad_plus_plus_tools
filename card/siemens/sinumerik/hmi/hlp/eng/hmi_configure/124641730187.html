Call

You can create up to two cameras for tracking remote processes and monitoring difficult-to-access areas.

Procedure

 

1.

Select the "Start-up" operating area.

 

2.

Press the "HMI" softkey.

 

3.

Press the ">>" softkey.

 

4.

Press the "Camera" softkey.

The "Camera configuration" window opens.

 

5.

Enter the camera parameters.

 

6.

Press the "OK" softkey to save the data.

 

 

If you press the "Cancel" softkey, all your changes will be rejected.

 

7.

Restart the HMI to activate the configuration.

 

8.

Press the "Test camera 1" and "Test camera 2" softkeys to test the configuration of the cameras.

Note

If you call the "Camera configuration" window during active streaming, you must restart the streaming process.
