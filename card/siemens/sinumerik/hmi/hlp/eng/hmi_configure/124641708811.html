Call

in the "Set up operating area" window.

Procedure

 

1.

Select the "Start-up" operating area.

 

2.

Press the "HMI" and "Operating area menu" softkeys.

The "Set up operating area" dialog box opens.

   

3.

Select the operating area that you want to configure.

 

4.

To configure softkeys 8 to 24, click the ">> Level" softkey once or twice.

 

5.

To edit the input fields, press the "Change" softkey.

 

6.

Select the operating area type under "Type", e.g. "VNC Viewer" or "Web Browser".

  

For the VNC Viewer you can use the "Details" softkey to display an expanded keyboard and the "Display VNC source" drop-down list to specify the area shown.

The "Details" softkey can only be operated if the "VNC Viewer" type is selected.

 

7.

Press the "Details" softkey in order to show the "Key transfer" area and blend in the "Display VNC source" drop-down list.

To hide these areas again, press the "Details" softkey once more.

 

8.

Make the necessary settings for the operating areas.

 

9.

Press the "OK" softkey to save the data.

 

 

If you press the "Cancel" softkey, all your changes will be rejected.

 

10.

Restart the HMI to activate the configuration.
