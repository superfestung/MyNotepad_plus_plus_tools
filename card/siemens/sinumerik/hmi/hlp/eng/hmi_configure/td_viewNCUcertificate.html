Displaying the NCU pairing certificate

Introduction

With the "View certificate" function, you can view detailed information about the pairing certificate of the NCU. You can safely manage the NCU pairing certificate in the SINUMERIK certificate store.

Requirement

  • With active user management: You have the role "Security Admin" or access level 2 (service).
    OR

  • Without active user management: You have at least access level 3 (user).

  • An NCU is in "Paired" status.

Procedure

  1. In the "Setup" operating area, call the overview page for pairing with the softkeys "Security > Pair IPC with NCU".

  2. Select an NCU with the status "Paired".

  3. Click on "View certificate".


    Pairing - viewing the NCU certificate

  4. The detailed information on the pairing certificate of the NCU is displayed.
