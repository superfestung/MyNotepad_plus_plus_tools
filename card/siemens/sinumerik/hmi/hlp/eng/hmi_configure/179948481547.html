Renewing pairing certificates

IPC and NCU/PU pairing certificates are only valid for a limited time.

  • Pairing certificates are valid for one year. A limited certificate validity time results in a higher level of security.

  • Pairing certificates are automatically renewed as soon as the particular certificate is to become invalid in the next 3 months or the validity of the certificate expires.

The certificate renewal process is also logged in the Security Eventlog.

Note

Automatically renewing pairing certificates has no influence on the pairing status of the paired devices!

Applications

  • The final validity of the pairing certificate has expired and the system was not restarted in the last 3 months.

  • The validity of the pairing certificate expires in the next 3 months.

  • The pairing certificates were corrupted.

  • Replacement scenario: Replacement of the IPC.

  • Replacement scenario: Replacement of the NCU.
