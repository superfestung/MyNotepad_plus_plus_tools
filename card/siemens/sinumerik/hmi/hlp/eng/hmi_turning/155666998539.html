Manufacturer cycle CUST_800.SPF for Y turning

There are explicit function marks in CUST_800.SPF.

_M90: Y turning: Initialization

_M91: Y turning: Selection: After aligning the tool and before approaching the starting position

_M92: Y turning: Selection: After approaching the starting position

_M95: Y turning: Deselection

With the orientation of the Y turning tool, the init mark "_M40:" is also passed through, and to position the rotary axes, mark "_M20:" of the CUST_800.spf is passed through.
