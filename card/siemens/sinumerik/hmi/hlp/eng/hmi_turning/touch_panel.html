Calibrating the touch panel

It is necessary to calibrate the touch panel upon first connection to the controller.

Note

Recalibration

If the operation is not exact, then redo the calibration.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the menu back key and the <MENU SELECT> key at the same time to start the TCU service screen.

 

2.

Touch the "Calibrate TouchPanel" button.

 

 

The calibration process will be started.

 

3.

Follow the instructions on the screen and touch the three calibration points one after the other.

 

 

The calibration process has terminated.

 

4.

Touch the horizontal softkey "1" or the key with the number "1" to close the TCU service screen.
