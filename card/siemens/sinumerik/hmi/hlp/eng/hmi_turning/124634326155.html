Function - thread centered

Function

Using the "Centered tapping" cycle, tap a righthand or lefthand thread at the center of the face surface.

During machining, either the main spindle or counterspindle rotates. You can alter the spindle speed via the spindle override; feedrate override is not operative.

You can select drilling in one cut, chipbreaking or retraction from the workpiece for stock removal.

The tool is moved with rapid traverse to the programmed position, allowing for the retraction plane and safety clearance.
