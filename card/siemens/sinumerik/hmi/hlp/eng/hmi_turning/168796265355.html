Geometry information: Overview

You can display geometry information for each point selected in the 3D model.

You can display the following geometry information:

  • Information about the points: to determine depth data (Z) for a contour (only X/Y coordinates)

  • Information about clearances: to determine the distance between 2 geometry elements

  • Information about angles: to determine the angle between 2 edges, for example to determine chamfer angles

  • Information about radius: to determine the radius, e.g. the radius of a circle segment
