Approach/retraction

  1. The tool approaches the starting point at rapid traverse at the height of the retraction plane and adjusts to the safety clearance.

  2. The tool moves to the machining depth FZ at the infeed feedrate Z1 and mills the characters.

  3. The tool retracts to the safety clearance at rapid traverse and moves along a straight line to the next character.

  4. Steps 2 and 3 are repeated until the entire text has been milled.

  5. The tool moves to the retraction plane in rapid traverse.
