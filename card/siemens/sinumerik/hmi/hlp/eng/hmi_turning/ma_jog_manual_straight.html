Straight turning

Use this function for simple, straight machining (e.g. face or longitudinal turning).

Procedure

 

1.

"Manual Machine" is active.

 

2.

Press the "Straight circle" softkey.

 

3.

Select the desired straight machining and press the "All axes" softkey

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the softkey "Straight X α"

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the softkey "Straight Z α"

 

4.

Specify the desired value for the feedrate F.

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the "Rapid traverse" softkey.

The rapid traverse is displayed in field "F".

 

5.

Enter the target position and, if required, the angle (α) for the axis or axes to be traversed.

 

 

Using the "Graphic view" softkey, you can toggle between the help screen and the graphic view in the screen.
