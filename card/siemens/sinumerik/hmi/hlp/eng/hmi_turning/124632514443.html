Approach/retraction

  1. The tool moves with G0 to safety clearance of the reference point.

  2. Travel to the final drilling depth with G1 and the speed and feedrate programmed before the cycle call.

  3. Dwell time at final drilling depth.

  4. Oriented spindle hold at the spindle position programmed under SPOS.

  5. With the "Lift" selection, the cutting edge retracts from the hole edge with G0 in up to three axes.

  6. Retraction with G0 to the safety clearance of the reference point.

  7. Retraction to retraction plane with G0 to drilling position in the two axes of the plane (coordinates of the hole center point).
