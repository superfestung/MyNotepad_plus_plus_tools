Calling the cycle

Procedure

 

1.

The ShopTurn program to be processed has been created and you are in the editor.

 

2.

Press the menu forward key and the "Straight Circle" softkey.

 
 

3.

Press the "Tool change point" softkey.

The "Tool change point" window opens.

 

4.

Press the "Accept" softkey in order to approach the tool change point directly.
