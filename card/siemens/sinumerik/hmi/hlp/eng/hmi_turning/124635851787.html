Calling up the cycle

Precondition

  • "programSYNC" option

Procedure

 

1.

The part program or ShopTurn program to be processed has been created and you are in the editor.

 

2.

Press the "Contour turning" softkey.

 

3.

Press the "Stock removal" softkey.

Only those parameters relevant for stock removal with 2 synchronized channels of a workpiece are subsequently described. All other parameters are described in Chapter "Contour turning".
