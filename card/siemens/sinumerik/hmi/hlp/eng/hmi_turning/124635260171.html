Calling the measuring version

Procedure

The part program or ShopTurn program to be processed has been created and you are in the editor.

 

1.

Press the "Meas. workpiece" softkey.

 

2.

Press the "Spigot" softkey.

 

3.

Press the "1 circular spigot" softkey.

The input window "Measure: 1 circular spigot" is opened.
