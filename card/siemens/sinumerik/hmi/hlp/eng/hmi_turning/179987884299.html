Displaying radius information

You can determine the following radii in the 3D model:

  • Radius of the circle segment: All elements that are circular with respect to the current plane can be selected.

Requirement

  • The required 3D model is open.

  • The list of machining steps is open.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the "Geometry info" softkey.

 

2.

Press softkey "Radius".

 

3.

Click on the circular edge in the 3D model for which you want to determine the radius.

The radius value data are displayed.

In addition to the radius value, the diameter and the center point are also displayed.
