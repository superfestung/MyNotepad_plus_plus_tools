Calling the cycle - displaying/hiding positions

Procedure:

 

1.

The part program or ShopTurn program to be processed has been created and you are in the editor.

 

2.

Press the "Drilling" and "Positions" softkeys.

 

3.

Press the "Line/Grid/Frame" or "Full/Pitch Circle" softkeys.

 

4.

Press the "Hide position" softkey.

The "Hide position" window opens on top of the input form of the position pattern.

The positions are displayed in a table.

The numbers of the positions, their coordinates (X, Y) as well as a checkbox with the state (activated = on / deactivated = off) are displayed.

The actual position in the graphic is highlighted in color.

 

5.

Using the mouse, select the required position and deactivate or activate the checkbox in order to hide the position or display it again.

In the diagram, skipped positions are shown in the form of a circle and displayed (active) positions are shown in the form of a cross.

Note: You have the option of selecting individual positions using the <Cursor up> or <Cursor down> keys – and hiding and displaying using the <SELECT> key.

Display or hide all positions at once

 

1.

Press the "Hide all" softkey to hide all positions.

 

2.

Press the "Show all" softkey to display all positions again.
