Simulation and simultaneous recording

For more complex machining processes, you can check the result of your inputs with the aid of the simulation, without having to traverse the axes (see Section "Simulating a machining operation"). The execution of the work steps is graphically displayed on the screen during this period.

 

Software option

You require the option "Simultaneous recording ShopTurn (real-time simulation)" for the simultaneous recording of the work steps.

Note

In "Manual Machine", you can simulate a work step with an already opened and filled out parameter screen form.

Setting up a blank shape

For the graphical display, a pre-defined blank shape is used. Just the same as under simultaneous recording, in automatic and simulation you can change the blank as required (see Section "Simulating a machining operation").
