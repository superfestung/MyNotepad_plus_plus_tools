Tapping with compensating chuck (G63)

When tapping with compensating chuck (G63) the spindle is not interpolated with the linear axis, but is operated in the closed-loop speed controlled mode. The feedrate of the linear axis dependent on the spindle speed and the thread pitch must be calculated and explicitly programmed. As the spindle and linear axis are not interpolated together, to compensate spindle speed fluctuations for example, a tapping drill in a length-compensating chuck is required.

Syntax

Meaning

G63

G63 programs tapping in the drilling direction up to the end of thread point as well as also the retraction motion (with inverse spindle direction of rotation!).

Effective:

Non-modal

X.../Y.../Z...

Drilling depth / end point of retraction motion

Traversing distance/position of the geometry axis (X, Y or Z) at the end of thread point or up to the end point of retraction motion

M3/M4

Direction of spindle rotation

M3

Clockwise rotation (right-hand thread)

M4

Counterclockwise rotation (left-hand thread)

Retraction motion must be programmed with the inverse spindle direction of rotation.

S...

Maximum permissible spindle speed while tapping

F...

Feedrate of the drilling axis

Feedrate to be programmed is obtained from the product of the spindle speed and the thread pitch of the tapping drill:

F = spindle speed * thread pitch

Note

After a G63 block, the last effective interpolation type G0, G1, G2 is active.

Note

Axis and spindle override

While the "Tapping with compensating chuck" function is active, the value of 100 % is active as axis and spindle override value.

Example

Tapping an M5 thread:

  • Spindle pitch according to the standard: 0.8 mm/rev

  • Spindle speed S: 200 rpm

  • Feedrate F = 200 rpm * 0.8 mm/rev = 160 mm/min.

Program codeComment
N10 G1 X0 Y0 Z2 F1000 S200 M3; Approach starting point; Spindle clockwise direction of rotation, 200 rpm
N20 G63 Z-50 F160; Tapping with compensating chuck; Drilling depth: absolute Z=50mm; Feedrate: 160 mm/min
N30 G63 Z3 M4; Retraction movement: absolute Z=3mm; Direction of rotation reversal; Spindle with counterclockwise direction of rotation, 200 rpm
