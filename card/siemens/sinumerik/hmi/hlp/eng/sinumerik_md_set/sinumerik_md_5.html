Explanations:
In the measuring cycles any intermediate positions are calculated prior to the actual set of measurements. These positions can be approached
- with collision detection (SD55740 $SCS_MEA_FUNCTION_MASK Bit0/Bit16=1 or
- without collision detection (SD55740 $SCS_MEA_FUNCTION_MASK Bit0/Bit16=0).
Depending on this setting different velocities are used for the approach:
- with collision detection (SD55740 $SCS_MEA_FUNCTION_MASK Bit0/Bit16=1):
With SD55634 $SCS_MEA_FEED_PLAN_VALUE the traversing feed is performed in the plane and
with SD55636 $SCS_MEA_FEED_FEEDAX_VALUE during traversing in the feed axis (applicate).
If the probe switches when these intermediate positions are approached, the movement is stopped and the alarm "Probe collision" is output.
- without collision detection (SD55740 $SCS_MEA_FUNCTION_MASK Bit0/Bit16=0):
The intermediate positions are approached with the maximum axis velocity (rapid traverse) in per cent as specified in SD55632 $SCS_MEA_FEED_RAPID_IN_PERCENT.
With SD55632 $SCS_MEA_FEED_RAPID_IN_PERCENT=0 and SD55632 $SCS_MEA_FEED_RAPID_IN_PERCENT=100 the maximum axis velocity is effective.
