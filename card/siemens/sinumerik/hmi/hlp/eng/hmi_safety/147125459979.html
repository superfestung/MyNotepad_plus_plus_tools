Calling the overview and detailed views

Procedure

 

1.

Select the "Setup" operating area.

 

2.

Press the menu forward key and the "Safety" softkey.

 

3.

Press the "Overview" softkey if the "Overview" setting area is not active.

  

Overview - calling the details

 

 

Press the "Details" softkey.

  

Overview - calling the checksums

 

 

Press the "Checksums" softkey.

 

...

 
  

To exit the detailed view or the view of the checksums: press the "<< Back" softkey.

The "Overview" window is displayed again.
