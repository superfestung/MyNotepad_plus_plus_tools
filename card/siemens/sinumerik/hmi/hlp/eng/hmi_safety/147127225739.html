Overview

The screen forms to use SafeUserData are provided in SINUMERIK Operate in the "Commissioning" operating area.

Requirements

The "SafeUserData" softkey is only visible if the following conditions are met:

  • You have initialized at least one SUD instance.

  • You have loaded the configuration into from the TIA Portal the controller.

  • You have set the "machine manufacturer" password.

Call

MENU SELECT > Commissioning > Menu forward key > Safety > Overview
