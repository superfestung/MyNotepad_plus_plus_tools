Overview of drives with SI telegrams

The telegram types for PROFIsafe and SIC/SCC for the respective drives/axes are displayed in the "Overview drives with SI telegrams" window.

An appropriate note is displayed for deviations between the settings of the NCK and the drive.

You can call the following diagnostic overviews from the "Overview of drives with SI telegrams" window:

  • PROFIsafe (the softkey is only active if, for the selected drive, a PROFIsafe telegram is configured)

  • SIC/SCC (the softkey is only active if, for the selected drive, a SIC/SCC telegram is configured)

To do this, use the softkeys in the vertical softkey bar.
