Setting the model quality

Precondition

Simulation is started.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the ">>" and "Model quality" softkeys.
The "Model quality" windows opens and displays the available values.

 

2.

Select the desired model quality.

 

3.

Confirm your selection with "OK".

For complex workpieces, switching the model quality can take some time. In this case, a progress bar will be displayed.
