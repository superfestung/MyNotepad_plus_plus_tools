Note

For the contour description, in conjunction with stock removal (CYCLE952), note the following:

After the contour description, if it is identified in the subsequent contour calculation that a programmed relief cut is not traversed along the relevant contour element as a result of the contour data or tool data, then the associated programmed element-related feedrate FB also does not become active.

Instead, machining is performed at this position corresponding to the calculated options without changing the feedrate.
