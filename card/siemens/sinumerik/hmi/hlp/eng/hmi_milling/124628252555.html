Angular milling

The basic effective direction can be selected via the axis direction keys or via the cross-switching lever. In addition, an angle (α1) can also be entered.

Procedure

 

1.

"Manual Machine" is active.

 

2.

Press the "Angular milling" softkey.

 

3.

Select the tool, spindle, and spindle direction and specify the machining feedrate.

 

4.

Enter the desired angle α1.

Note

Selecting/deselecting the angular milling and changing the angle α1 is only possible in the reset state.
