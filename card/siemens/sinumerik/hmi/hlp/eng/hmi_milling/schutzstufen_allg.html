Definition of access levels

Access to programs, data and functions is user-oriented and controlled via eight hierarchical protection levels. The protection levels are subdivided as follows:

  • Four password levels for system, machine manufacturer, commissioning engineer and user

  • Four keyswitch positions for users

Protection levels extend from 0 to 7:

  • 0 is the highest protection level

  • 7 is the lowest protection level
