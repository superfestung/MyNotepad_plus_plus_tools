Supported models

The following drilling, milling and turning elements are identified when evaluating the CAD file on the NX server:

  • Blank

  • Face milling

  • Face milling with an edge

  • Centering

  • Drilling

  • Centered drilling

  • Reaming

  • Deep-hole drilling 1

  • Deep-hole drilling 2

  • Tapping

  • Centered tapping

  • Rectangular pocket

  • Circular pocket

  • Thread milling

  • Rectangular spigot

  • Circular spigot

  • Longitudinal groove

  • Circumferential groove

  • Open groove

  • Contour milling (contour, pocket)

  • Groove
