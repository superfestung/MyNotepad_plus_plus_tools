Activate collision avoidance

Precondition

  • Collision avoidance is setup and an active machine model is available.

  • The setting "Collision avoidance" has been selected for the AUTO operating mode or for the JOG and MDA operating modes.

Procedure

 

1.

Select the "Machine" operating area.

 

2.

Press the <AUTO> key.

 

3.

Press the "Sim. rec." softkey.

 

4.

Press the "Other views" and "Machine space" softkeys.

  

During simultaneous recording, an active machine model is displayed.
