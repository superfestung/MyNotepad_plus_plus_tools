Calling the cycle - stock removal in JOG

Requirement

To carry out simple stock removal of a workpiece in manual mode, a measured tool must be in the machining position.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the "Machine" operating area key

 

2.

Press the <JOG> key.

 

3.

Press the "Machining" and "Stock removal" softkeys.

 

 

 

 

4.

Enter desired values for the parameters.

 

5.

Press the "OK" softkey.

The parameter screen is closed.

 

6.

Press the <CYCLE START> key.

The "Stock removal" cycle is started.

 

 

You can return to the parameter screen form at any time to check and correct the inputs.
