Plan view

Display as a top view

 

1.

Simultaneous recording or the simulation is started.

 

2.

Press the "Top view" softkey.

The workpiece is shown from above in the top view.

Changing the display

You can increase or decrease the size of the simulation graphic and move it, as well as change the segment.
