D

  • Maximum depth infeed for insertion – (only for ∇ and ∇ + ∇∇∇)

  • For zero: Insertion in a cut – (only for ∇ and ∇ + ∇∇∇)

D = 0: 1. cut is made directly to final depth T1

D > 0: 1st and 2nd cuts are made alternately to infeed depth D, in order to achieve a better chip flow and prevent the tool from breaking, see approaching/retraction when roughing.

Alternate cutting is not possible if the tool can only reach the groove base at one position.
