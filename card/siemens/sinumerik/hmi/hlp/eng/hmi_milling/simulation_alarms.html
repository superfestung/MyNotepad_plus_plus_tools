Displaying simulation alarms

Alarms might occur during simulation. If an alarm occurs during a simulation run, a window opens in the operating window to display it.

The alarm overview contains the following information:

  • Date and time

  • Deletion criterion

    Specifies with which softkey the alarm is acknowledged

  • Alarm number

  • Alarm text

Precondition

Simulation is running and an alarm is active.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the "Program control" and "Alarm" softkeys.

The "Simulation Alarms" window is opened and a list of all pending alarms is displayed.

 

 

Press the "Acknowledge alarm" softkey to reset the simulation alarms indicated by the Reset or Cancel symbol.

The simulation can be continued.

 

 

- OR -

 

 

Press the "Simulation Power On" softkey to reset a simulation alarm indicated by the Power On symbol.
