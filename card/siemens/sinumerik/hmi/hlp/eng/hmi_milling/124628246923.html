Simple workpiece machining

In "Manual Machine", you machine workpieces directly in the "JOG" mode without creating a program.

Functions

The following functions are available to you for machining in manual mode:

  • Axis movements

  • Angular milling

  • Straight (face and longitudinal milling) and circle

    Note

    Tool, spindle speed and direction of spindle rotation are activated with <CYCLE START>.

    A change in feedrate immediately becomes active.
