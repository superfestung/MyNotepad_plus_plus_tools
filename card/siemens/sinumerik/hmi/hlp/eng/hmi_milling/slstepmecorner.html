Note

The four measuring points or the measuring path DFA must be selected so that contour within the total path: 2 · DFA [in mm] is reached. Otherwise, no measurement will be able to be made.

Internally in the cycle, a minimum value of 20 mm for the measurement distance DFA is generated.
