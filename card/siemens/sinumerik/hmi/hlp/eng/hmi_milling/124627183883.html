Programming a circle with known center point

The tool travels along a circular path from its actual position to the programmed circle end point. You must know the position of the circle center point. The control calculates the radius of the circle/arc on the basis of your interpolation parameter settings.

The circle can only be traversed at machining feedrate. You must program a tool before the circle can be traversed.

Procedure

 

1.

The ShopMill program to be edited has been created and you are in the editor.

 

2.

Press the menu forward key and the "Straight Circle" softkey.

 

3.

Press the "Circle center point" softkey.
