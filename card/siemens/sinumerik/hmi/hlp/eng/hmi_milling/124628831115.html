Starting the "SINUMERIK Integrate" software

You can integrate the machine into the company's processes using the "SINUMERIK Integrate - AMB, AMC, AMM" software.

Requirement

The "SINUMERIK Integrate" softkey was configured.

 

Machine manufacturer

Please observe the information provided by the machine manufacturer.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the <MENU SELECT> key.

 

2.

Press the menu forward key.

 

3.

Select the "SINUMERIK Integrate" operating area.

The "SINUMERIK Integrate" window opens.

Further information

For information on configuring the softkey, refer to the SINUMERIK Operate Commissioning Manual.
