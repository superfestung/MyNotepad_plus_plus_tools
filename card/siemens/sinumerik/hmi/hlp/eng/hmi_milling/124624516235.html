Rotating the view

You can change the orientation of the drawing.

Requirement

The DXF file is open in the Program Manager or in the editor.

Procedure

 

1.

Press the "Details" and "Rotate figure" softkeys.

 
 

2.

Press the "Arrow right", "Arrow left", "Arrow up", "Arrow down", "Arrow clockwise" or "Arrow counter-clockwise" softkey to change the position of the drawing.
